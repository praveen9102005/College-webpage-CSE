Celebrating Excellence

Our department takes pride in the accomplishments of our students, faculty, and alumni

Our students secured first place in the National Coding Championship, competing against 50+ universities. The team developed an innovative algorithm for optimizing renewable energy distribution.

Secured a $2M grant from TechCorp for collaborative research in cybersecurity and IoT. This grant will fund the development of next-generation security protocols for smart cities.

August 2022 Research Grant
Principal Investigator: Dr. Emily Roberts

Dr. James Wilson received the Best Research Paper Award at the International Conference on AI for his groundbreaking work on explainable artificial intelligence in healthcare diagnostics.

Our team won the National Hackathon with their innovative healthcare monitoring solution. The project uses machine learning to predict patient health deterioration 24 hours in advance.

January 2022 Faculty Recognition

November 2021 Student Award
Award Recipient: Rachel Green
